Look, I'm tired of watching brilliant developers build amazing things that never make a penny.

You know the story. You spend six months crafting the perfect app. It's technically beautiful. The code is clean. The features are solid. But somehow... nobody pays for it. Sound familiar?

Here's what I've learned after watching (and living through) too many failed projects: we don't fail because we can't code. We fail because we don't know how to turn code into cash.

That's why Indie10k exists.

## What We're Actually Building

Right now, Indie10k helps you go from $0 to your first $1000 with daily tasks that actually matter. Not busy work. Not generic "build your audience" advice. Specific actions that move the needle on revenue.

But that's just the beginning.

We're building toward something bigger: an AI-powered co-pilot that knows your project, understands your goals, and gives you exactly one thing to work on each week. No overwhelm. No analysis paralysis. Just focused execution.

Think of it as the business co-founder you wish you had—one that's actually good at the business stuff.

## The Big Picture

Our vision is simple: turn Indie10k into the complete income assistant for indie makers. From that first spark of an idea all the way to sustainable $10k+ monthly revenue.

Because here's the thing—indie developers don't need another productivity app. They need a partner that helps them finish what they start, launch without overthinking, and actually win.

That's what we're building. One focused mission at a time. 🚀
